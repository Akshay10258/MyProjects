TSUNAMI

Tsunamis are ocean waves produced by earthquakes or underwater landslides or undersea valconic erruptions.



CAUSES:

  • Earthquakes.
  • Valconic Erruptions.
  • Underwater explosion
  • Landslides.
  • meteorite impacts



EFFECTS:

  • Loss of life and mass injuries.
  • Damage and destruction of homes and businesses, ports and harbors, cultural resources, utilities, and critical infrastructure and facilities
  • There may be loss of access to basic services such as power, sewer, and water



PREVENTION:

  • Listen to emergency information and alerts
  • Be alert to signs of a tsunami, such as a sudden rise or draining of ocean waters.
  • Get to high ground as far inland as possible.
  • Evacuate: DO NOT wait!



PREPARATION:

  • Stay out of the water and away from beaches and waterways.
  • Get more information from radio, television, and your mobile device.



RESPONSE:

  • Move to high ground or inland(Away from the water).
  • If you are near the coast and experience shaking from an earthquake: DROP, COVER, and HOLD ON to protect yourself.



RECOVERY :

  • Stay out of buildings if water remains around them.
  • When re-entering homes or buildings, use extreme caution as floodwaters may have damaged buildings.
